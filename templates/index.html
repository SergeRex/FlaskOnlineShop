NewOldWatches is a watch art studio specializing in customizing vintage (and also modern) mechanical watches since 2005.

We convert pocket watches to wrist watches, create new dials, build new cases, skeletonize movements, engrave movements / cases / dials / buckles / heads and do more other works on the field of the watch restoration.


The catalogue of watches that have found their lucky owners earlier placed in our watches collection.

On our site you will find also the catalogue of some of the watch parts (handmade dials, custom cases, straps, ...) that has been created by our team .

We offer for sale watches, that have been completed already, so you can buy it immediately, just watch out our watch shop.
